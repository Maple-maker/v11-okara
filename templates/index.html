How to Use (Form Fields):

  1. 1. Generate DD1750 - Upload BOM and template, click Generate.
  2. 2. Download PDF - Save the items-only PDF to your computer.
  3. 3. Open your Filled DD1750 Template - In Adobe Acrobat Pro, Mac Preview, or any PDF editor.
  4. 4. Fill in Admin Fields - Type in Unit, Packed By, Date, Requisition No., Order No., etc.
  5. 5. Save Template - Save your filled template.
  6. 6. Combine (Manual) - Open your items PDF, drag it on top of your saved template, and save the combined PDF.

Note: This method keeps your admin form fields 100% editable and correct!
