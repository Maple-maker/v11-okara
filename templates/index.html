New! Auto-Combine Feature

Our previous tool had issues with PDF form fields. We've added a fix using pdftk, a standard PDF toolkit that handles form fields correctly.

Instructions:

  • Fill in your DD1750 template (Unit, Packed By, Date, etc.) and SAVE IT.
  • Check the "Auto-Combine with Template" box below.
  • Click Generate.
  • Download the PDF.
  • Open in Adobe Acrobat Pro (or Preview/Acrobat on Mac).
  • Place the generated items PDF on top of your filled template.
  • Save the combined PDF.
Note: If "Auto-Combine" is checked, we attempt to merge automatically. If unchecked, you just get the items list to print manually. This ensures the admin section on your template stays intact.
